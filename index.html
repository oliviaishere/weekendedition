Questions

Where is it?

Rua 1º de Maio 48, Foros de Albergaria, Alcácer do Sal, Setúbal — a little over an hour's drive south of Lisbon. For GPS accuracy, search "Country house with 5 bedrooms surrounded by nature" on Google Maps.

How do I get there?

For those interested, Olivia will book a car transfer — costs split between everyone who joins. Pickup at 4 PM from a central Lisbon location, and a return trip at 11 AM from the house back to central Lisbon on Sunday. Expect somewhere between €30–60 per person for the round trip, depending on how many people join. Otherwise, drive yourself or coordinate an Uber.

Is there a shop nearby?

The closest big shop is a 15-minute drive (an ALDI). I recommend buying food and drinks on the way. Olivia will likely have a car to make runs as needed. Plan ahead!

How much is it?

The accommodation is taken care of. Please contribute food and drinks — there will be a big barbecue on Saturday night. The optional car transfer from Lisbon is the one extra cost, split between whoever joins (roughly €30–60 per person round trip). Message the group if you’re interested in the car transfer and I’ll confirm prices before booking.

What else should I bring?

A swimsuit and towel for the pool, plus sunscreen for my fellow pale friends. It's the Alentejo summer, so expect sunny hot days. Otherwise, good vibes, food, drinks, games for Friday, and music suggestions.

Do I have to share a bed?

Maybe. Most bedrooms have a king bed; one bedroom has two singles. I've assembled people into what I think is the most reasonable assortment of rooms. Feel free to switch.

I can't come for the full weekend — can I join for part of it?

Absolutely. The big day is Saturday, so even if you can't stay over every night, come for as much as you can. A wider group of friends will also be invited to join just for the day on Saturday.

What happened to the Survivor theme?

Maybe next year! Physical and mental competition in the summer in the desert I think ended up being against the spirit of a chill pool party weekend. You are all safe and won't be voted off the island.

Are more people coming?

Yes! More friends are joining for Saturday. The overnight crew is sorted, and a wider group will be coming for the day on Saturday.

Can I bring a plus one?

Maybe. To stay overnight, likely not. Otherwise, check with Olivia.
